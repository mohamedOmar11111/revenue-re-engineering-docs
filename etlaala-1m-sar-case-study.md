# CASE STUDY: THE ETLAALA REVENUE PIVOT

## 1. THE DEFICIT (-70,000 SAR / Month)
In Q2 2025, the target entity was facing a significant revenue deficit due to:
*   Fragmented human management.
*   Low-intent lead acquisition.
*   Static, non-responsive creative assets.

## 2. THE INTERVENTION (MARKETING ENGINEERING)
The "Campaign" approach was scrapped in favor of a **Modular Revenue Terminal**:
*   **Automated Intent Classification:** Replaced manual screening with a GPT-4o-mini powered swarm.
*   **Bayesian Creative Strike:** Continuous testing of 100+ high-status visual hooks.
*   **CTR Benchmark:** Achieved a peak 14.3% CTR on core regional keywords.

## 3. THE RESULT (+1,000,000 SAR Net Profit)
Within 12 months, the entity transitioned from a deficit to a seven-figure profit milestone, maintaining a high-status brand identity throughout the expansion.
